Nam Jim Suki (น้ำจิ้มสุกี้)

Asian Ingredients With N

Thai Sauce Instant Product
Table of contents
  • Usage

Thai Suki sauce short for Sukiyaki sauce, or in Thai “Nam Jim Suki” ( น้ำจิ้มสุกี้ ) is a red to brownish sour-spicy sauce used for Thai hotpots and other dishes.

The name Sukiyaki is borrowed from the Japanese hotpot dish of the same name, which has a completely different flavor profile. The main ingredient of the sauce, however, comes from Chinese cuisine - it is red fermented tofu.

Thai Sukiyaki sauce is available as an instant product from various manufacturers, but you can also prepare it yourself relatively easily.

You can also make Thai Suki sauce yourself using my recipe for Home­made Nam Jim Suki.
